more about holistic gospel ministries

our vision

Our Vision is to see Jesus lifted up and to see Him drawing all people to himself by the Power of the Holy Spirit.

our mission

Redemption of people and places through holistic evangelism and discipleship withthe Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ.

our objectives

  • We seek to lift the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, in various ways so that He can draw all people to himself
  • To equip Christian disciples for life and service for the glory of God
  • To partner with other Christian ministries to fulfill the great commission
  • To provide social service and support to the general community with the compassion of Christ
